Foods that I love

Gumbo

One of my favorite foods is Gumbo. I'm from Louisiana and Gumbo is a native dish. There are so many different ways to make a Gumbo, and many of those ways are very delicious. There's a right and a wrong way to make gumbo, and usually, a louisiana native can quickly distinguish between the two. The right way is if it tastes delicious. And if it does not, then it is absolutely wrong and should be discarded immediately.

BBQ

BBQ is my second favorite food. I love the smoked flavor of juicy BBQ fresh off the grill. I love BBQ burgers, ribs, pork steak, t-bone steak, and just about anything from a wood or charcole grill. I love BBQ!!

Pizza

Pizza is my third favorite food. I can eat just about any pizza as long as it tastes delicious. I love fast food pizza as well as home made pizza. I can eat a lot of pizza. My favorite pizza is a Meat Lovers. My second favorite pizza is supreme. I love pizza!!!
